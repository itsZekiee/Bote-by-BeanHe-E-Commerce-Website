HOUSE BLEND

BOTE by Beanhe offers two distinct house blends: Columbia Subpremo, a base of fully washed Excelso and Supremo Colombian coffee beans from small to medium family-owned farms in the "coffee-triangle" region, and Brazil, Sultan, a harmonious blend of smooth Brazilian coffee beans and locally-sourced Sultan beans, creating a rich and balanced flavor experience.
