BOTE by BeanHe offers Gourmet Tuyo in a Glass Container. The name "BeanHe" comes from "Bean" (literal bean) and "He" (God). Initially a clothing business idea, it shifted to a coffee shop due to the pandemic. With 10 years of experience, the business has been growing for 4 years and continues to thrive.

BOTE by BeanHe's first coffee product was "Cold Brew". They started with a kiosk-style store in Cay Pombo, moved to a standing store in Sta Clara, returned to Cay Pombo with an actual store after a year, and finally settled in Pulong Buhangin near PUP SMB after another year.
